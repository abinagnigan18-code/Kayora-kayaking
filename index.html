About Kayaking

Kayaking is an exciting water sport where you paddle through rivers, lakes, or oceans using a small boat called a kayak.

In Kochi, kayaking offers a unique experience through mangroves, calm backwaters, and scenic beauty. It is suitable for beginners and professionals alike.
